Our Mission

The Cook Foundation is dedicated to uplifting Wisconsin communities through generous giveaway programs. We support students with school supplies, households with essential goods, businesses with resources, and individuals in need — because everyone deserves a chance to thrive.

Our Giveaway Programs

Students

Backpacks, school supplies, laptops, and educational resources.

Households

Food, clothing, furniture, and household essentials.

Businesses

Startup kits, equipment, and growth resources.

Individuals

Emergency aid, job support, and personal development items.
