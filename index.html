Meet Edwin Castro

Edwin Castro is a visionary leader dedicated to lifting people up. Through The Uplift Program, he channels his resources and passion into creating real opportunities for students, families, and aspiring entrepreneurs.

His mission is simple yet powerful: to provide the tools, resources, and support needed for individuals to rise above challenges and build better futures.
