From Lottery Winner to Community Builder

After winning $22 million from the Wisconsin Lottery in 2020, Thomas Cook made a life-changing decision — to use his blessing to help others. Instead of luxury, he chose legacy. The Cook Foundation was born from gratitude and a deep desire to support hardworking families across Wisconsin.

Today, the foundation provides direct financial help for education, housing, medical needs, small businesses, and emergency support — with no middlemen and no complicated process.
