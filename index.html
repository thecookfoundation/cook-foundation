Our Founder’s Story

On June 10, 2020, Thomas Cook won $22 million from the Wisconsin Lottery. Instead of keeping the fortune for himself, he chose to create something lasting. Together with his family, he founded The Cook Foundation — a promise to give back to the communities that raised him.

What began as one man's life-changing moment has grown into a beacon of hope for students needing school supplies, families struggling to make ends meet, small businesses fighting to survive, and individuals facing hardship. Every giveaway, every act of kindness, carries the spirit of gratitude and compassion.

— Thomas Cook, Founder
